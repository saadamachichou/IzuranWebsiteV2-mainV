Simple list of services you can put on invoices.

## Main Invoice Items

### Products
- **Physical Products** - Vinyl, Merch, Clothing, Accessories
- **Digital Products** - Digital Downloads, Digital Files

### Tickets
- **Event Tickets** - Early Bird, Second Phase, Last Phase, VIP

### Fees & Services
- **Shipping Fee** - For physical product delivery
- **Delivery Fee** - For COD orders
- **Processing Fee** - Payment processing charges
- **Service Fee** - Ticket service charges

## Example Invoice Line Items

**Product Order:**
- Product Name (Qty) - $XX.XX
- Shipping - $X.XX
- **Total - $XX.XX**

**Ticket Sale:**
- Event Name - Ticket Type (Qty) - $XX.XX
- Service Fee - $X.XX
- **Total - $XX.XX**

**COD Order:**
- Product/Ticket Details - $XX.XX
- Delivery Fee - $X.XX
- **Total - $XX.XX**

---

**Note**: All prices support multiple currencies (default: USD)
